Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI categorisation?
Our AI achieves 95%+ accuracy after the first month of learning your business patterns. It continuously improves as it processes more of your transactions, handling complex scenarios that generic software struggles with.
Does it work with my existing Xero setup?
Yes, TaxFlow integrates seamlessly with Xero. We sync your transactions, categorise them intelligently, and push the results back to Xero while maintaining your existing chart of accounts.
What if I need help from my accountant?
Our Growth and Scale plans include accountant collaboration tools. You can grant your accountant access to review and approve returns, while they benefit from the time saved on data entry and categorisation.
Is my financial data secure?
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and comply with New Zealand privacy laws. Your data is stored securely in New Zealand data centres and never shared with third parties.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, there are no lock-in contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time and retain access until the end of your billing period. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.
